OUR STORY

Started in 2021, Sash Mandi Darbar has been serving exceptional cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our chef brings over 10 years of international experience to create dishes that blend traditional techniques with modern innovation.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every dish tells a story of passion, craftsmanship, and respect for the ingredients.
